# Sound design files (optional)

Drop royalty-free sound effects here and the pipeline mixes them in automatically:

- `whoosh.*` (mp3/wav/m4a/flac/ogg) — swells into every segment transition
- `impact.*` or `hit.*` — cinematic hit on the hook at 0:00

Volume and timing live in `config.yaml` under `sfx:`. Empty folder = sound design skipped.
Good free sources: pixabay.com/sound-effects, mixkit.co (check licenses for monetized use).
